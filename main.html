Physical Futures

// Join the registry  ·  Bridging the gap between artists & engineers

Physical Futures is a initiative of high-agency artistswho design future-forward objects that center the physical body.

  • Physical Futures is not a spectator sport. Every attendee contributes and is actively creating.
  • All Physical Futures projects must be material objects and revolve around the human body.
  • Physical Futures is for engineers wanting to imbue more craftsmanship into their work, and artists who want to refine their technique.
  • Physical Futures is a VC & scout-free space. No VCs.
  • Bring a material project you've made, or a project you want feedback on–– we love supporting WIPs. We're here for all of it as long as it's physically futuristic.
  • Not sure if your work counts? Message one of us!

// OUR PREROGATIVE

𓏵 ARTGINEERING HAS RESULTED IN
SOME OF HUMANITY'S BEST WORK.
IN AN AGE THAT OFTEN PRIORITIZES DIGITAL ARTIFACTS, IT'S IMPERATIVE PHYSICAL ARTISTS AND STEM DISCIPLES WORK EVEN MORE CLOSELY AS WE DESIGN FUTURE-FORWARD PHYSICAL OBJECTS THAT EXIST BETWEEN THE WORLDS OF ART, MANUFACTURING, FASHION, AND TECHNOLOGY.

// About

No startup pitches. No panels. Five-minute demos, then conversation.
Our community is interdisciplinary, and intergenerational. All generations are welcome here.
Curated over open. Every attendee is doing something genuinely strange and physical.
Disciplines collide. Fashion meets optical engineering. Jewelry meets robotics.
The physical human body is the through-line. Everything we make touches it.
