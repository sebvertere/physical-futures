Physical Futures · Kickoff

Photoreactive

The inaugural edition brought together fashion designers, jewelers, sculptors, and optical engineers for a night of demos around photoreactive and light-responsive materials. Small, intentionally curated, high-signal — with conversation that went long after the demos ended. The hypothesis was tested. It worked.
